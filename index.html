Join The KEMET CLUB

A BAF MEMBERS-ONLY MEETING & GATHERING SPACE!

The BAF is excited to introduce our new members-only meeting and assembly space, the Kemet Club! The Kemet Club is open 7 days a week from 7am-10pm for BAF Members and their guests. The purpose of the Kemet Club is to provide a safe-space to meet and assemble in fellowship among people who share our consciousness and vision. Those who have been BAF members in good standing for at least 6 months are eligible to receive a key for convenience, as well as reserve the Club for private events. All live events at the Kemet Club serve as fundraisers for the Kemet Club and our Real Estate Investment Fund.

KEMET CLUB AMENITIES:

Complimentary coffee, tea, hot cocoa, and spring water 65" TV with YoutubeTV, Netflix, HBOMax and Disney+ Wifi and house Android and I phone chargers 3-in-1 Printer/Copier/Scanner with copy paper Bluetooth party speaker with karaoke and mic Refrigerator, dual microwave/air fryer 2 Ring lights Propane grill, outdoor seating (Spring-Fall)

KEMET CLUB RULES:

1. No smoking.

2. No BYOB (Bring your own alcohol). Alcohol is served at live and private events.

3. Maximum of 3 guests per visit. (You cannot invite the same guest twice. They must be a member the second time the visit. You are responsible for your guests' actions and behavior.)

4. Keep the Club clean. Please make sure you clean up behind yourself (cleaning supplies are located in the break area under the cabinets).

5. Purchase all items from the Kemet Club Store, not the BAF Campaign Store!

6. Do not leave without paying for your purchase.
